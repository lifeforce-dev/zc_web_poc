1. Burn Ability

Finalized

Three states: Targeting (orbiting flames), Primed (fire wall surrounds), and Burning (firenado collapse). Based on fire-4c-wildfire-firenado. The burning animation loops automatically every few seconds.

Burn Behavior:
  • Targeting: Orbiting flame wisps circle the number like a forming tornado
  • Primed: Full fire wall surrounds the number from all edges
  • Burning: Fire spins into a firenado that collapses to center, value decreases by 1
